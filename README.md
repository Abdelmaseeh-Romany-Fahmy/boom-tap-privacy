Privacy Policy for Boom Tap
Last Updated: February 20, 2026

At Boom Tap, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

1. Information Collection
Our application does not require users to create accounts or provide personal information such as names, emails, or phone numbers directly to us.

2. Third-Party Advertising (Unity Ads)
To keep the game free, we show advertisements provided by Unity Ads. These third-party services may collect and use certain data to serve and measure ads, including:

Device Identifiers: Advertising ID (OAID / GAID).

Device Information: Device type, model, and Operating System (OS) version.

Network Information: IP address and Network type (e.g., Wi-Fi, 4G).

Usage Data: Ad interactions and gameplay performance.

This data is used solely to provide relevant ads, prevent fraud, and analyze ad performance. You can view Unity's Privacy Policy here: Unity Privacy Policy.

3. Data Sharing and International Transfer
We do not sell your data. However, the technical data mentioned above is disclosed to Unity Technologies (a third-party provider) and may be transferred to and processed in servers located outside your country of residence.

4. Children's Privacy
Boom Tap is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. All ads served are filtered to be appropriate for the selected age rating.

5. Consent
By using our game, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the AppGallery contact information.
