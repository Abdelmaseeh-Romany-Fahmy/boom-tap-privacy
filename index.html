Privacy Policy for Boom Tap

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

At Boom Tap, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

1. Information Collection

Our application does not require users to create accounts or provide personal information such as names, emails, or phone numbers directly to us.

2. Third-Party Advertising (Unity Ads)

To keep the game free, we show advertisements provided by Unity Ads. These third-party services may collect and use non-personal data, such as:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID).
  • Device type and OS version.
  • Game play data and ad interactions.

This data is used solely to provide relevant ads and analyze ad performance. You can view Unity's Privacy Policy here: Unity Privacy Policy.

3. Children's Privacy

Boom Tap is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. All ads served are filtered to be appropriate for the selected age rating.

4. Consent

By using our game, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the AppGallery contact information.
